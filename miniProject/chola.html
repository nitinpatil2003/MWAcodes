Chola Dynasty Administration

Introduction

The Chola Dynasty was known for its efficient and well-structured administrative system. It had a well-defined hierarchy of governance.

Central Administration

The king was the supreme ruler and had ministers to assist him. Important positions included the prime minister and commanders.

Provincial Administration

The empire was divided into mandalams, which were further divided into valanadus and nadus. Each region had local governance.

Local Administration

Village administration was highly organized. The system of local self-government, such as the ur and sabha, was remarkable.
