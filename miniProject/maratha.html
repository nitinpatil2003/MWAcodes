Maratha Dynasty Administration

Peshwas: The Prime Ministers

The Peshwas played a crucial role in the administration of the Maratha Empire. They were responsible for governance, military campaigns, and diplomatic relations.

Revenue System

The Marathas implemented an efficient revenue system, collecting taxes such as Chauth and Sardeshmukhi. These helped in strengthening the empire's economy.

Military Organization

The Maratha military was well-structured with cavalry, infantry, and naval forces. The use of guerrilla warfare tactics made them formidable in battle.
